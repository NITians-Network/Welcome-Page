An Initiative by Project COMET, IIITians Network

ABOUT US

Project COMET is an initiative by IIITIAN's Network to promote collaborative learning among IIITIANs. In our glorious journey of eight months, IIITians Network have come up with ample of things. A number of events, resources and opportunities to learn are brought to everyone by our team. In this journey, we've evolved ourselves from nowhere to somewhere. Now we're extending our ventures to make technical communities of IIITians and for IIITians.

GOALS

Collaborative Learning Hardcore Discussions Hackathon / Competitions Guidance for Beginners Industrial Collaborations Startups Upliftment Sharing Resources Attracting Investors Upskilling IIITians

How will this community work

  1. There will be several sub communities under this
  2. As of now we're starting the community on Discord/Telegram
  3. For each communit there will be a seperate group/channel
  4. Communities will be run by team leaders and moderators
  5. Leaders would be top skilled people in various technical fields, while moderators would be interested students in one or more fields, interested to learn new things

SUB COMMUNITIES

Artificial Intelligence Big Data Blockchain Cloud Computing Data Science Robotics Competitive Coding Web Development Cross Platform Game Development Ethical Hacking Design and Animation Freelancing Entreprenurship Management

Role of Team Members

  1. The purpose of making this community is to decentralize the community as well as to maintain relevancy of content
  2. The team members will have full responsibility of maintainig / promoting their community
  3. Team spirit and sinceirity is important. Team will work like a family
  4. Their initial responsibility would be to give an intial push to the community
  5. A leaderboard will be available on the website ranking moderators and team leaders according to engagement in their community

How to Join

  1. Forms are circulated for making the team leaders and moderators, until this site is in construction.
  2. Channels and group links are being circulated in the technical societies of each IIIT
  3. The early bird membership is limited. Later on every member has to verify their identity as a IIITian.
  4. Selection of leaders and moderators will be on an Interview basis

BENEFITS OF JOINING

Entreprenurship and Leadership Skills Technical Skills Team Work Find people with similar goals Network with other IIITians
